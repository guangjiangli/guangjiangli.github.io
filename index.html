About me

I now work in Richard Temkin's group in PSFC at MIT studing on THz gyrotron amplifier, laser-driven semiconductor switch for THz resonator ring, and the THz permitivity of Zirconia for DNP-NMR. In Aug. 2021, I completed PhD jouney in Lyubov Titova's group in Worcester Polytechnic Insititute, I focused on photoinduced carrier dynamics in 2D materials (MXenes) and solar energy mateirals using Time resolved Terahertz spectroscopy. I worked as a research assistant at Institute of Solid-State Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences from 2015 to 2017. I obtained master's degree on condensed matter physics from University of Science and Techonolgy of China in 2017. Outside of lab, I like playing basketball, hiking, swimming. I am also a photography enthusiast, I ocassionally shot with film camera.
